Trouva Design Principles

We design collaboratively

Work with others to design better. We involve front-end developers, product managers, and the wider team members in the design process and drive better outcomes together. Make design open.

We do this by:

  • Talking regularly about our goals and processes
  • Asking for and giving regular feedback
  • Sharing your findings with others to spread the knowledge

We design for all users

Trouva's products are used around the world by a broad community of boutique owners and customers. Good design is open to all, and we follow a set of standards to make sure our products are context-aware, welcoming, simple to use and universally accessible.

We do this by:

  • Following best practices for design
  • Understanding how and where our users access our products
  • Taking iterative steps to improve the accessibility of our products for users with disabilities

We leave assumption at the door

Start by taking a step back. We take the time to understand the wider context and explore the problem before diving into the solution. We design from data, insight and evidence to build products that make an impact for our users.

We do this by:

  • Talking to stakeholders to understand business goals
  • Talking to our users regularly and observing them user our products
  • Using data and analytics to gain quantitative feedback
  • Releasing iteratively to get insight fast
  • Conducting user research to educate ourselves
  • Questioning everything and not accepting problems at face value

We do our best work now

We respect our future selves by asking the hard questions so we can solve the right problems. We take the time to do every job well, no matter how small or how complex, and when we need to adapt we are the guardians of quality.

We do this by:

  • Challenging the status quo: innovation doesn't come from nowhere
  • Adapting quickly, but carefully
  • Iterating on our own designs
  • Doing less, but better
  • Striving for simplicity
  • Making the goals easy to achieve
  • Reducing design and development debt by working with consistency

We build community

For our boutiques, our customers, and ourselves; we're here to foster togetherness. Design brings people together, and a Trouva, we're more than just a marketplace.

We do this by:

  • Involving our boutiques in what we do
  • Being open and sharing our work with the world
  • Helping foster unique bonds between our boutiques and customers
