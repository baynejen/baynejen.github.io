Get me to the church!

It is a fairly early service (1030am start) so we recommend that if you are travelling by train that you come the night before. However we totally appreciate this may not be possible so no pressure! If you do want to travel on the morning of, here is some travel information...

Travelling by train

The nearest train station to the Church in Heckmondwike is Batley train station. Trains go from Leeds at ’26 and ’56 past the hour, there are taxis available from Batley station but it’s advisable to book one in advance.

Alternatively, there are trains which also go to Dewsbury which isn’t too far away either. They go from Leeds at ’09, ’26, ’36 and ’57 past the hour.

Travelling by air

The nearest airport to the service is Leeds / Bradford international. Alternatively, there’s Manchester, which is about an hour’s train ride or two hours car journey.
