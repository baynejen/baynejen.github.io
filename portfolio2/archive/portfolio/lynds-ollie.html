Lynds and Ollie: Wedding Website

UX, UI, HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP

I was delighted to be asked to make a wedding website for two of my best friends.

The brief

This site needed to contain a lot of content, so it was crucial that the architecture was precise and simple. To allow the couple to send lightweight invitations, this website would contain all information the guests would need to know, from important timings and bridal party details, to accomodation, travel and gift registry information. Most importantly, it should allow guests to RSVP and stipulate any dietary requirements. The couple wanted the design of the site to stand out from traditional wedding websites and reflect their fun loving personalities.
