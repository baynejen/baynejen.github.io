About me

I'm a versatile and passionate Product Designer with over nine years experience in high growth eCommerce startups. I'm currently working as Senior Product Designer at Trouva, a VC-funded marketplace for independent boutiques.

My experience ranges from design for B2B systems to consumer ecommerce products, to design systems.

Design for growth

I'm driven to deliver solutions that have impact for both the user and the business. My design process begings with a deep understanding of the problem I'm trying to solve, and is shaped by continuous learning, iteration and testing.

Full-stack design

Both results-driven and detail-orientated, I like to get involved in all phases of the product cycle, from opportunity mapping and scoping, through to development. I'm used to collaborating closely with engineers to deliver quality, consistent and scalable products. I bring strong experience in design research and user-centered thinking to the product teams I work in.
