About Me

I'm a versatile and passionate Product Manager and UX/UI designer with strong eCommerce and startup experience. With a proactive attitude and excellent teamwork skills, I’m naturally detail orientated, constantly curious, and have a ‘make things happen’ attitude. Fresh from 6 years at Artfinder, I'm seeking new challenges and opportunity.

A wearer of many hats at Artfinder, I worked closely with engineering, marketing, support and bus dev to gather user insight and data, prioritise features, build product specs, and design solutions to drive growth. With a flair for both iterative development and ‘big picture’ thinking, achievements included introducing AB testing and usability testing as core processes, integrating personalisation into the UX, redesigning the search interface, developing key features such as commissions, framing, visual recognition search and in-context visualisation, and building an entire CRM flow. I also managed a total company rebrand over a four month period.

Through active participation in sprint planning and PR reviews, I’ve developed good technical awareness and learn fast. Skilled at leading group brainstorms to kick-start ideas, I bring momentum to every project I’m involved in and have successfully established a cross-departmental ‘tribe’ culture.

Born in Edinburgh, based in London.
