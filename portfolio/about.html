About Me

Originally from Edinburgh, I've been working in the digital world in London since 2010. I'm now lucky to be part of the small team at Artfinder, a VC funded startup that is creating an entirely new, democratic and affordable market for art by connecting buyers directly with artists online for the first time.

As Product Manager and lead designer, my job is to ensure that Artfinder meets and exceeds the needs of both our customers and artists, across all web platforms. My aim is to build an intuitive product that's a delight to use, and which is optimised for conversion at every step. I believe in an iterative approach to design, using A/B testing, user research and data insight to help shape the product growth. This user-centric design philosophy underpins my UI and UX work.
