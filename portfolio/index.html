Hello and welcome to my Portfolio. I'm a London-based (but Edinburgh at heart) Product Manager, UI/UX designer and front-end developer. You'll find some of my recent projects below!

Memory Box

www.memory-box.me

A personal project to archive the hundreds of tickets I've accumulated (hoarded) over the years. A great way to remember the amazing gigs, exhibitions and places I've been lucky to go to.

Alasdair Bayne | Portfolio

www.alasdairbayne.com

A portfolio site to showcase the work of Alasdair Bayne, filmmaker and editor. Jen was asked to create a website that could display Alasdair's personal projects alongside his professional work, making use of both visual and video content.

Lynds and Ollie Getting Married

www.lyndsandolliegettingmarried.com

I was delighted to be asked to make a wedding website for two of my best friends. Built using html, css, javascript and php, this site is the destination for all invitees to RSVP to the wedding and find out everything they need to know about the big day.

Scotch Egg Diaries

-- Coming soon --

A personal project still in progress, this site will document my mission to taste and rate every scotch egg in London. A project I'm hugely looking forward to getting underway with. Expected launch April 2016.

Sforzando

-- Coming soon --

Jen was asked to create a portfolio website for Douglas and Maddy who together make up Sforzando, one of Edinburgh's finest piano/organ and vocal duos. This site will combine audio, video and visual content and is designed to reflect their classical style. Expected launch April 2016.

Alexander Mead

-- Coming soon --

Jen was aksed to create an academic portfolio for Alexander Mead, who is currently holding a post-doctoral fellowship at the University of British Columbia. Alexander wanted a site that could easily showcase his research interest and previous publications. Expected launch March 2016.
