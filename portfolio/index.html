Hello and welcome to my Portfolio. I'm a London-based (but Edinburgh at heart) Product Manager, UI/UX designer and front-end developer. You'll find some of my recent projects below!

Memory Box

www.memory-box.me

A personal project to archive the hundreds of ticket stubs I've accumulated (some might say hoarded) over the years. A great way to remember the amazing gigs, exhibitions and places I've been lucky to go to. Built as part of the General Assembly Web Design Circuits course I took in 2015.

Artfinder

www.artfinder.com

My current role is Product Manager at Artfinder, the VC funded art marketplace. Working closely with a team of developers, I am responsible for both shaping the product roadmap and for UI/UX design. I also manage all HTML email design and print/digital advertising.

Alasdair Bayne | Portfolio

www.alasdairbayne.com

A portfolio site to showcase the work of Alasdair Bayne, filmmaker and editor. I was asked to create a website that could display Alasdair's personal projects alongside his professional work, making use of both visual and video content.

Sforzando

http://www.sforzando-live.com

I was asked to create a portfolio website for Douglas and Maddy who together make up Sforzando, one of Edinburgh's finest piano-organ and vocal duos. This site combines audio and visual content and is designed to reflect their classical style.

Alexander Mead

www.phas.ubc.ca/~mead/

I was asked to create an academic portfolio for Alexander Mead, who is currently holding a post-doctoral fellowship at the University of British Columbia. Alexander wanted a site that could easily showcase his research interest and previous publications.

Scotch Egg Diaries

Lynds and Ollie Getting Married

www.lyndsandolliegettingmarried.com

I was delighted to be asked to make a wedding website for two of my best friends. Built using html, css, javascript and php, this site allows all invitees to RSVP to the wedding and find out everything they need to know about the big day. They wanted the design of the site to stand out from traditional wedding websites and reflect their fun loving personalities.
