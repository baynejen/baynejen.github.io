Artfinder graphic report

Print, Graphic

In 2017 Artfinder produced an Independent Art Market Report to shed light on the lives of independent artists today. I was by our PR team to bring their data and findings to life with a graphic report. My aim was to pull out the key data so that the reader could easily read through the rport. I used Artfinder's brand red to highlight essential details and bring a visual consistency to the 31-page report.
