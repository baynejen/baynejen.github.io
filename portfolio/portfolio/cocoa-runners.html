Cocoa Runners

Email

Cocoa Runners is an eCommerce startup specialising in subscription boxes for artisan chocolate.

The brief

I was asked by the founding team shortly after their launch in 2014 to build their email communications. Time was of the essence, and the business required templates and designs for drip campaigns as well as weekly newsletters. I worked with the founders and the Marketing team to establish which content the emails would need to display.

The process

Mailchimp was the tool of choice. Using their style guide product photography, I sketched some ideas for a boilerplate template that could be used across all campaigns. From here, I began creating the boilerplate in Mailchimp, working fist on the header and footer and body styling to make sure the template reflected their brand and was suitable across a variety of campaigns. I then built a series of reusable snippets that could be swapped in and out.

Testing

The templates needed to undergo rigorous testing before they could be used, in order to ensure they would work across all email clients and devices. My tool of choice has always been Litmus. Once the template was bulletproof, the drip campaigns were set up using Mailchimp Automation, and a master newsletter template was created for the Marketing team to use. Using Mailchimp, and working with their style guide, I designed and built a series of responsive drip campaigns (welcome and subscriber conversion emails), as well as boilerplate templates to use for their newsletter email communications.
