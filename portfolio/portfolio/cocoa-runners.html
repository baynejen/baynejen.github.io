Cocoa Runners
Email

Cocoa Runners is an eCommerce startup specialising in subscription boxes for artisan chocolate. I worked with the founding team shortly after their launch in 2014 to build their email communications. Using Mailchimp, and working with their style guide, I designed and built a series of responsive drip campaigns (welcome and subscriber conversion emails), as well as boilerplate templates to use for their newsletter email communications.
