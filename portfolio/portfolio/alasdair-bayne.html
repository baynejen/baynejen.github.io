Alasdair Bayne

UX, UI, HTML, CSS

Alasdair Bayne is an award-winning filmmaker and editor based in London. His work has been shown at Raindance Film Festival, Edinburgh International Film Festival, and has received BAFTA New Talent awards.

The brief

I was asked to create a website that could display Alasdair's personal projects alongside his professional work, making use of both visual and video content. It should be easy to navigate on desktop and mobile, and should have contact information.

The process

I began with some wireframe work to establish a consistent structure that could be used across all projects. Alasdair has a variety of content, from documentary filmmaking to storyboarding and VFX work, so I worked to create a site structure that could accomodate all this in an intuitive way.
