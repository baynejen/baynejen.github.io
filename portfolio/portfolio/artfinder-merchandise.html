Artfinder artist merchandise

Print, Graphic

In partnership with MOO, I created a series of branded business cards, leaflets and postcards that artists on Artfinder could customise with their name, Artfinder URL and artwork images. I worked with the team at MOO to ensure the templates would be easy to customise on MOO's platform, and with Artfinder's Marketing team to produce a series of promotional images of the materials to encourage artists to make a purchase on MOO.
