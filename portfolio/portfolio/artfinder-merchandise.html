Artfinder artist merchandise

Print, Graphic

In partnership with MOO, I created a series of branded business cards, leaflets and postcards that Artfinder artists could customise with their name, Artfinder URL and artwork images on the MOO platform. I worked with the team at MOO to ensure the templates would be easy for the artist to customise, and with Artfinder's Marketing team to produce a series of promotional images to encourage artists to buy.
